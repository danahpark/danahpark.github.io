Welcome to my website! I am a fourth-year graduate student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, where I am supported by Stanford Gradudate Fellowship and Kwanjeong Scholarship. At Stanford, I work with Prof. Ali Mani and my current research involves computational modeling of complex flow systems such as separated wall-bounded turbulent flows and the investigation of physics therein.

At the same time, I am working with Prof. Adrian Lozano Duran from Massachusetts Institute of Technology and working on understanding turbulence structure and energy cascade using transformative tools.

As an undergraduate, I graduated from Seoul National University in 2017 with a B.S.E. in Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering. During my undergraduate studies, I worked with Prof. Shin Hyung Rhee and I studied multi-phase flows and computational fluid dynamics (CFD) particularly for naval applications.

I am currently looking for an internship opportunity in the field including but not limited to CFD, machine-learning, data-science, and scientific computing. Please feel free to contact me via email. Also, feel free to check out my research.
